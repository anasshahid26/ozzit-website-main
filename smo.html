Social Media Optimizations

Facebook Services

Social Media is not a FAD, its HERE to stay! It's the way forward and your competition is already using it. In Pakistan: 35 million Internet users 8 million Facebook users (main social media site) This is your target market as they have the highest disposable income.

Facebook Page Management

Digibuz will create a Facebook page for your brand as well as a user profile and take care of all activity on your page. Let us take care of all your social media activity!

Concept & Copy Writing

In order to communicate with your audience you need interesting concepts and ideas. The daily posts made by us will engage your already existing clients as well as increase your number of fans. The content posted will be presented in a simple, easy-to-understand way and be intelligent, engaging, informative and most importantly value-adding. We will provide a direct relationship with your clientele and answer any queries related to your product instantly.

Creative Design for Page

Design is an extremely important element for Facebook activity as that is what catches the users attention first. Using minimal text and maximum aesthetic appeal we aim to catch the users eye and grab their attention towards your product. Being experts at what works well on Facebook and what doesn't, our designers have got you covered!

Facebook Advertising

The first and foremost step to build your fan page is Facebook advertising. In order to be effectively communicating we need to first ensure we have a constantly increasing audience. Facebook Ads are highly targeted by city, gender, age, likes & dislikes so it ensures we're advertising to the right people. Our expertise in this department ensures we design an Ad that will call for our targeted audience to click on it and this click will convert into a LIKE on your Facebook page. Once you get that like all future activity on your page will show up on that user's Facebook newsfeed.

Page Update

Using Facebook sponsored Ads we will increase your fans and number of likes. Once users have already liked your page it is our job to keep them constantly engaged and post fun and exciting content to constantly remind them about your brand. We will make one post daily and this will show up on your users newsfeed. If they like the post it will show up on their friends newsfeed and broaden your marketing spectrum without any cost. These daily post updates have to be well thought out, interesting and up to date with the times. You can leave all that to us!

Tab Development

Being active on Facebook decreases the need for a website. Facebook tabs give your page a personalized touch and are developers will create whatever required tabs for your page. For example a separate tab for your MENU if you are a restaurant, a list of services if you are a salon, a price list if you are a designer or anything that you may require.

Social Strategy & Client Services

Planning & ensuring we achieve our desired result after implementing a tailor made strategy for your brand management. Communicating effectively with your clients, on your behalf at all times and keeping you up to date with suggestions /complaints /deliveries etc.
